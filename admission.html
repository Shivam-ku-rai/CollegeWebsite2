M. Sc. programmes:

The School offers two programs of M. Sc. Life Sciences and M. Sc. Industrial Microbiology of two year [four semesters ] duration. The courses are designed to ensure a sound theoretical and practical training in the first three semesters The students are sent to do a project work in the fourth semester in any national laboratory/ university department/ Industry. The common basic courses covered in M. Sc. Life Science /Industrial Microbiology include biochemistry, cell biology, genetics and molecular biology, instrumentation, basic microbiology, immunology, biostatistics, and bio-informatics. In addition to these M. Sc. Life Science has additional courses in plant physiology, tissue culture, animal physiology and environmental biology. Additional courses in M. Sc. Industrial Microbiology include fermentation technology, down stream processing, food and medical microbiology, virology and microbial metabolism



PhD - Doctoral Programmes:
Doctoral Academic Programme:

The School offers opportunities for doctoral and post doctoral research in the following branches of life science. In the research programs students having M.Sc.in any branch of Life Science / Biological science are admitted throughout the year. The candidates are required to qualify in NET/GATE exams before making an application for either a direct award of fellowship by UGC /CSIR or for fellowships in research schemes awarded to individual faculty members. At present about twenty-five research students are working in the department.
