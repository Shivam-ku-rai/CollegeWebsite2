Welcome To School of Life Science

Infrastructure

Labs:

School of Life Science is presently homed in two buildings ; the third floor of Vigyan Bhavan and an annex building. The labs are supported by infrastructure facilities like animal houses, plant growth chambers, tissue culture lab, microbiology lab, botanical garden etc. A theoretical paper on instrumentation is part of the course supported by practical training on the research applications of these instruments.



Equipments:

The department is well equipped with latest instruments used in biological research. The major instruments available are EPR, HPLC,GC, Fluorescence spectrophotometer, UV-Vis spectrophotometer, Dual wavelength spectrophtometer, Ultra centrifuge, High speed refrigerated centrifuges, lab fermentor, Industrial fermentor, industrial centrifuge, Chemi imager, Phase contrast microscope, Trinocular research microscopes with video recorders. PCR, Electrophoresis and gel documentation systems etc.



Library:

The School has a departmental library with over thousand latest books related to the courses. The department also provides computer facilities with internet for M.Sc. and Research Students.
