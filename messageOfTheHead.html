Welcome To School Of Life Science

Message of the Head:

School of Life Sciences, DAVV, Indore is one of the few university teaching departments(UTDs) that is running Postgraduate teaching and research program in Life Science in India. It was started to expose and train the students primarily to/in advance areas of Life Sciences that include Molecular biology, Genetic, Biochemistry, Immunology, System Physiology, Plant Physiology, Environmental Biology, Biodiversity, Biostatistics and Tissue culture. Keeping in mind the Industrial requirements, we are also offering M.Sc., Industrial Microbiology program. Our primary aim is to generate trained manpower to meet the country's demand. In fact, over the years, Life Science& Microbiology program sat DAVV,Indore have been established as leading academic programs both from teaching and from research point of view. This has been possible primarily because most faculty members of the School are internationally recognized for their contribution to basic and applied aspects of research in Life Sciences/Microbiology. Many of our alumni are now in good positions in India & in abroad. I believe anyone who gets admitted in our UTD will immensely benefit & with his /her sincere efforts he/she can achieve his/her goal. I welcome all the students to School of Life Science and wish them a bright future.

Prof(Dr).Anand Kar
